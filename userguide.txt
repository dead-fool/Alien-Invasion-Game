How to play:

- Shoot the aliens using spacebar.
- Dont let the aliens reach the bottom.
- Dont let the aliens touch the ship.
- Use arrow keys (or A = left, D = right) to move the ship sideways.
- When there are no ships available, the game is over.
- Press 'Q' to quit and exit out of the game anytime.
- Press 'Backspace' to go back to home screen from game or highscorescreen.
- Press 'Esc' to pause or unpause
